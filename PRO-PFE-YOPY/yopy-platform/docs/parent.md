
**Dashboard

Color Palette
The image uses a cool, slightly muted, and moody color palette, dominated by the gradient on the shirt and the stark contrast of the black tattoos.

Deep Indigo / Navy Blue: The top portion of the t-shirt.

Muted Plum / Burgundy: The bottom portion of the t-shirt, created by the gradient effect.

Pale Peach / Desaturated Tan: The skin tone of the character.

Inky Black: Used heavily for the crisp line art, the deep shadows under the chin, and the striking zebra print tattoo.

Cool Grays / Slate: Seen in the background elements, adding to the slightly moody, low-light atmosphere.

Art Style
Webtoon / Manhwa Style: The artwork is highly characteristic of modern Korean webtoons or digital comics.

Digital 2D Illustration: Created using digital drawing software.

Cel-Shading with Gradients: It utilizes flat colors with hard-edged shadows (cel-shading) to define the folds in the clothing and the anatomy, combined with a smooth gradient effect specifically on the fabric of the shirt.

Clean Line Art: The drawing relies on distinct, confident black outlines to define the character and the intricate details of the tattoos (like the petals of the sunflower and the zebra stripes)
